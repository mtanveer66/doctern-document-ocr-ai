# Doctern — Use Cases

Real-world problems Doctern solves.

## Invoice & Receipt Processing

Accounting teams receive invoices in dozens of formats. Doctern extracts line items, totals, dates, and vendor details into structured data — no retyping, no template setup per vendor.

## Form Digitization

Paper or PDF forms — applications, intake sheets, surveys — become structured records. Useful for operations, HR, healthcare intake, and government workflows.

## Financial Document Extraction

Bank statements, balance sheets, and financial reports often arrive as PDFs or scans. Doctern turns their tables back into spreadsheet data analysts can actually work with.

## Logistics & Shipping Paperwork

Bills of lading, packing lists, and customs documents are table-heavy. Doctern preserves row-and-column structure so quantities, codes, and descriptions stay aligned.

## Data Migration & Digitization Projects

Organizations digitizing historical archives can use Doctern to convert large volumes of scanned documents into searchable, structured data.

## Embedded Document Intelligence

Software companies can use Doctern as the document-extraction layer inside their own products, instead of building OCR and table parsing from scratch.
